CSCE606 Project

  • Project Name: NearBy.
  • Project Member: Sicheng Wang, Yi-Wei Chen, Kwei-Herng Lai, Ting-Hsiang Wang, Xiaotian Han, Kaixiong Zhou.
  • Project Customer:Chien-Ping Chen.
  • Project Github:https://github.com/YIWEI-CHEN/nearby .
  • Project Introdution: With increasing demand in senior care, we aim to develop a platform offering peer-to-peer in-home care service. Our system will be able to match care-seeker and provider according to their locations and preferences. For example, the care provider can specify the preferred location and time, and the care taker can narrow down the services they want and indicate the desired details about the care provider. The care providers are trained practitioners who are willing to earn some money by providing professional care services in their spare time. The care seekers are usually elders or people under medical conditions who need on-demand healthcare assistance.

    • heroku git:remote -a <your-app-name> - associate the Heroku app with the repository
